Support & Contribution

You can make an impact.

.As a charity the Museum relies on donations. Gifts support the care, preservation and display of the collection, and make the inspirational work of our curators, conservators, scientists and educators possible. Large or small, every donation helps secure the future of the collection. We are truly grateful for your support.

How your money can help

Revealing object stories Donations enable the world class researchers at the British Museum to use new techniques to uncover the hidden histories of objects in the collection.
